About us

At Indus International School Belagavi, each student's potential is fulfilled in an atmosphere encouraging curiosity, creativity, and growth. IAIS provides a distinctive educational experience in Belagavi with its contemporary facilities and international student community. Our maximum class size of 25 facilitates interactive learning, and our teacher-to-student ratio 1:9 guarantees individualized attention. With state-of-the-art facilities and a comprehensive approach to teaching, we are dedicated to fostering your kid's development. IAIS offers a complete educational experience that any parent may be proud of, and boarding facilities are offered. Join in and observe how your child flourishes in an engaging setting.
